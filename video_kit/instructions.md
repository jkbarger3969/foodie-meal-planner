# Video Generation Kit Instructions

This kit contains everything you need to generate a professional marketing video using AI tools like HeyGen, Synthesia, or Descript.

## What's Inside?
1. **`video_script.md`**: The exact script for your AI avatar to read.
2. **`mockup_*.png`**: High-quality UI mockups of your app (Generated by me).
3. **`broll_meal.png`**: A delicious food shot for the ending.

## How to specific Generate the Video (Example: HeyGen)

1. **Log in** to HeyGen (or similar tool).
2. **Create Video**: Select "Landscape" video.
3. **Choose Avatar**: Pick a "Lifestyle" or "Chef" looking avatar.
4. **Copy Script**: Copy the lines from `video_script.md` into the text box.
5. **Add Visuals**:
   - At the 5-second mark, upload `mockup_pantry.png` as a background.
   - At the 15-second mark, upload `mockup_ai_chef.png`.
   - At the 45-second mark, upload `broll_meal.png`.
6. **Generate**: Render the video (usually takes 5-10 mins).

## Tips
- **Voice**: Choose a voice that sounds energetic and slight fast-paced.
- **Music**: Add a "Lo-Fi Hip Hop" or "Upbeat Corporate" background track at 10% volume.
