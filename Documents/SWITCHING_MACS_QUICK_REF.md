## Switch Between Macs

1. **Open iOS app Settings**
2. **Change IP address** to different Mac
3. **Tap "Save & Connect"**
4. **Done!** Now connected to different Mac

## Keep Both IPs Handy

Save in iPhone Notes:
```

            192.168.1.100 (iPad)

            192.168.1.105 (iPad)
```

## Common Scenarios

**Use Your Mac for Planning:**
- Connect to your Mac's IP
- Plan meals, send shopping list
- Shop and sync back

**Use Wife's Mac for Cooking:**
- Change to wife's Mac IP
- Get her recipes on iPad
- Cook using her meal plan

**Switch Anytime:**
- Just change the IP in settings
- No data loss
- Both Macs keep their own data
